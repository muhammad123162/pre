About Us


Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a nurturing and dynamic learning environment that empowers students to grow intellectually, emotionally and socially. We are committed to excellence in education, character development, and preparing our learners to thrive in a constantly changing world.


Our History

Established in 2025, MB International Academy began with a simple goal: to make quality education accessible and meaningful. From our humble beginnings with few classrooms and passionate teachers, we have grown into a thriving institution known for academic excellence, innovation, and community involvement. Over the years, we've remained dedicated to shaping future leaders who carry knowledge, empathy, and confidence beyond the classroom walls.


Our Values

At MB International Academy, we value honesty- doing what's right, even when no one's watching. We grow together by learning, helping others and always doing our best. We show respect by treating everyone with kindness and fairness. We believe in always trying our best and never giving up. we stay curious, ask question, and love learning new things. And we grow stronger by helping one another and working as a team.
